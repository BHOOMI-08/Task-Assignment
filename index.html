About CodeCraft 2025

CodeCraft 2025 is a 48-hour hackathon that brings together students, developers, and innovators to create groundbreaking solutions to real-world problems. Whether you're a seasoned developer or just starting your coding journey, this event offers the perfect platform to learn, collaborate, and showcase your skills.
